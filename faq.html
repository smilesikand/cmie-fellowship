FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

1. What is the duration of Fellowship? 

The CMIE Fellowship will typically run for 1 year. After the completion of 1 year, the Fellow and the government will mutually take a call on extending the duration by an another year.

2. What is the age limit to qualify for the Educate Delhi Fellowship?
If on June 15th 2016 your age is between 22 to 27 years you qualify to apply. In case of SC/ST category, your should be between 22 to 29 years of age. Those born after 16th June 1994 cannot apply.

3. I am studying B.B.A 3rd year, can I apply?
Those who have completed graduation/post graduation on or before June 15th 2016 and have a minimum 24 months of work experience can apply.

4. I’m a student. have done internships/volunteered in NGO’s, will this be considered as relevant work experience?
Internship/volunteer work in social or public organizations will be considered as work experience only if it was a full-time work, done for an aggregate 18 months or more.

5. How many fellows will be finally selected for 2016-17 batch?
We are looking forward for forty fellows. Additional five fellows will be on a wait list.

6. Can I expect a stipend during fellowship? 
Fellows will get a monthly stipend of 40,000 INR.

7. If selected, can I continue working with other organisation?
The CMIE Fellowship is a full-time opportunity. Fellows are prohibited from being employees/consultants for any other organisations during fellowship.

8. Can I go on leave during fellowship?
Yes you will be allowed to take leaves with prior permission. The details will be shared during induction after joining the Educate Delhi Fellowship.

APPLICATION DETAILS

The application for Delhi Fellowship closes on June 15, 2016 and the batch of selected fellows would begin from July 15, 2016.
