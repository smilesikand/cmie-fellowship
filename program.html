DESIGN


INDUCTION
A week long induction program to learn about Delhi’s education set-up, immerse with peers from diverse backgrounds and cultures


LEADERSHIP CONCLAVES
Series of dialogues with leaders such as Hon'ble Chief Minister of Delhi, Education Minister, Director (Education) to learn about policies and practices to impact public education


REFLECTION FORUMS
Week long course at Jeevan Vidya Camp and fortnightly reflection sessions with Peers to introspect, learn from the challenges while engaging with complex situations 


SOCIAL IMPACT CANVAS
Platform to contemplate and develop the plan to impact and bring  about change in social systems


LEAD THE CHANGE
Associate and work with key stakeholders of Public Education System for a year to impact learning transformation in Delhi’s schools

FRAMEWORK

The Educate Delhi Fellowship is rooted in principles of constant action, guided reflection and regular mentoring to prepare Changemakers of tomorrow.
